About Flavored Watr Inc.

Refreshment reimagined, the award-winning flavored bottled water crafted with purity, creativity, and sustainable sourcing in every bottle.

Our Mission

At Flavored Watr Inc., our mission is to make hydration delicious, simple, and responsible. We craft small-batch flavored water that uses natural flavors, responsibly sourced ingredients, and recyclable packaging, so you can hydrate with confidence and taste.

  • Purity: Ultra-filtered water foundation, nothing artificial in the water itself.
  • Flavor-first: Natural botanical and fruit essences for subtle, delicious flavor.
  • Planet-aware: Recyclable packaging and local sourcing reduce our footprint.

What We Make

A lineup of artisanal flavored water: Lavender Lemon, Cucumber Mint, Blood Orange Sage, and seasonal limited editions. All formulas are low-calorie, naturally flavored, and designed for everyday drinking.

Sourcing & Sustainability

Water is filtered to the highest standards and our flavor essences come from local growers whenever possible. We use 100% recyclable bottles and partner with local recyclers and packaging innovators to reduce waste.

Distribution & Partnerships

We sell direct-to-consumer, supply regional cafes and markets, and partner with catering companies for events. Interested retail partners can contact partnerships@flavoredwatr.example.
